SANITATION DEPARTMENT — SERVICES, CONTACTS & COMPLAINT PROCEDURES
==================================================================

DEPARTMENT OVERVIEW
-------------------
The Sanitation Department (also called Solid Waste Management Department) of the Municipal Corporation is responsible for keeping cities clean and managing waste collection, transportation, processing, and disposal.

SERVICES PROVIDED
-----------------
1. Daily door-to-door garbage collection (residential and commercial)
2. Street sweeping and cleaning
3. Operation of transfer stations and landfills
4. Drain and nullah (storm water drain) cleaning
5. Biomedical waste management coordination
6. Construction and demolition waste removal
7. Night soil removal and septic tank cleaning
8. Management of public toilets and community toilets
9. Special cleaning drives during festivals

GARBAGE COLLECTION TIMINGS
----------------------------
- Residential areas: 6:00 AM – 10:00 AM (may vary by zone)
- Commercial areas: 5:00 AM – 9:00 AM
- Hospitals and bulk generators: As per agreement

TYPES OF WASTE BINS
--------------------
GREEN BIN: Wet/Organic waste (vegetable peels, food waste, leaves)
BLUE BIN: Dry waste (plastic, paper, metal, glass)
RED BIN: Hazardous/domestic hazardous waste (batteries, medicines, sanitary waste)

COMPLAINT CATEGORIES — SANITATION
-----------------------------------
1. Garbage not collected for more than 2 days — MEDIUM priority
2. Overflowing garbage bins on streets — HIGH priority
3. Large garbage dump/pile in public space — HIGH priority
4. Burning of garbage in open area — CRITICAL (health hazard + fire risk)
5. Dead animal on road — HIGH priority (health hazard)
6. Public toilet not functional / unclean — MEDIUM priority
7. Construction debris blocking road — HIGH priority
8. Illegal dumping of waste in vacant plot — MEDIUM priority

HOW TO REPORT SANITATION ISSUES
---------------------------------
Phone:
- Swachh Bharat Helpline: 1969
- Municipal Sanitation Control Room: varies by city (check corporation website)

Online:
- Swachh City App (Google Play / App Store)
- swachhbharaturban.gov.in → Citizen Grievances
- Corporation website → Sanitation Complaint section

In Person:
- Visit nearest Ward Office
- Meet the Ward Sanitary Inspector (WSI)

RESOLUTION TIMELINES (AS PER SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT)
------------------------------------------------------
- Garbage not collected: Must be collected within 24 hours of complaint
- Overflowing bin: Cleared within 6 hours
- Dead animal removal: Within 4 hours
- Drain/nullah cleaning: Within 48 hours (non-monsoon season)
- Public toilet repair: Within 72 hours

IMPORTANT CONTACTS — SANITATION
--------------------------------
Swachh Bharat Mission Helpline: 1969
Swachh City App: Available on Google Play and App Store
Municipal Solid Waste Management Cell: Contact your city corporation
National Green Tribunal (NGT) Helpline (for open burning etc.): 011-23070935

PENALTY FOR OPEN DUMPING
--------------------------
Under Solid Waste Management Rules 2016, open dumping of garbage can attract fines ranging from ₹500 to ₹25,000 depending on the quantity and nature of waste.
